Low carb tortilla chips

Ingredients needed

  • Almond Flour
  • mozarella shredded cheese
  • Parchment paper
  • stove-top pan

Steps

  1. Mix 3/4 of almond flour and one cup of shredded mozarella into bowl
  2. Mix and microve in bowl for 30 seconds
  3. grease pan with a little olive oil
  4. mix bowl and microwave for another 30 seconds
  5. Knead into a flat dough that fits the pan and heat on medium until edges are brown
  6. flip as needed. top will be slightly brown when done. ETA 10 minutes.
  7. cut into small triangle chips. Will harden when cool. Enoy!
