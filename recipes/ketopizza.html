Keto Pizza

Low carb pizza ..mmmm
  • Almond flour
  • Shredded mozarella cheese
  • Pizza sauce
  • Parchment paper

  1. Mix 3/4 almond flour and 1 cup of mozarella cheese
  2. Mix in microwave safe bowl and put in microwave for 30 seconds twice, each time mixing moire
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Mix up the almond flour and cheese into a dough and knead over pizza pan with parchment paper down, or else it will stick.
